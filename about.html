Event driven architectures using Apache Kafka, MS Event Hub, MS IoT Hub, RabbitMQ, MQTT Brokers. Also interested in improving collaboration, build automation, containerization, orchestration, quality, CICD and everything DevOps. When I am not providing clients with solutions to their toughest problems, I am teaching the next generation of software engineers at New Brunswick Community College in Moncton.
